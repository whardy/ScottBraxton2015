Stephen Scott

Presidential candidate Stephen Scott is a junior political science and multidisciplinary studies double major, with a concentration in leadership studies, communication studies, and history. Scott is from Shepherdstown, West Virginia. Since his enrollment at West Virginia University, Stephen has been actively involved in Student Government Association, where he has served as an intern, governor, and the vice chair. His work in SGA has covered a range of issues including: student support services, diversity and inclusion efforts, academics, advising and retention rates. In addition to SGA, Stephen has been actively involved in other areas around campus, especially the residence halls, where he has served as a member of Student Conduct Board, served as secretary and parliamentarian for the Residence Hall Association and served as a resident assistant.
